Our Story

About us

In 1982, a vision for hospitality was born in the heart of Mr. V.N. Hariharan, a seasoned professional in the industry. This vision took shape with the esteemed Haco Restaurant, which laid the groundwork for what has become today, the Vallam Restaurant at Kallancheri, Kumbalangi.

A Feast for the Senses

Our menu features a delightful range of freshly prepared seafood delicacies, caught straight from the pristine waters nearby. From mouthwatering prawn roasts to succulent fish curries, each dish is crafted with love and care, ensuring a taste of Kerala's coastal flavors with every bite. Every meal is an experience, made with the finest ingredients to bring you the best of our local cuisine.

Table Booking is a Must!

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply seeking a peaceful get-together with family, colleagues, or your loved ones, Vallam promises a unique experience where nature, culture, and delicious food come together. To ensure we provide you with the best possible service, table booking is essential. We are committed to delivering a memorable dining experience, and your reservation helps us serve you better.
